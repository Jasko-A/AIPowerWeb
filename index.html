PowerPoints Token--PPS

PPS is issued per renewable energy project. PPS can be purchased with Ethereum/fiat currency. The amount that gathered will be used to fund the project itself. The holder of PPS of a project is therefore entitled to receive green energy services.










How is PPS Priced?







Note: PPA, power purchase agreement, is always signed before the project construction. It defines the price of the electricity when project construction is finished and starts generating energy. Therefore, the invester bidding price will always be lower than PPA price.
